Steps to open the website on your phone: 
1. Make sure that you on the same network.
2. `ctrl` + `shift` + `~` to open the terminal.
3. type `ipconfig` and hit enter.
4. copry `IPv4 Address` it will be something like that `192.168.43.109`.
5. go to browser, Replace `127.0.0.1` with IP that we got from above steps.
